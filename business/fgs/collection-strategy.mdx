## SEO Collection Page Formula

Each collection page should follow this structure:

1. **Hero section** — Emotional headline + lifestyle image
2. **Collection description** — 150-250 words, naturally incorporating the target keyword 3-5x
3. **Product grid** — Sort by bestseller, with personalization options visible
4. **Gift finder widget** — Filter by recipient, occasion, budget
5. **Social proof strip** — Review count + star rating + "As seen in"
6. **FAQ section** — Target long-tail variants (e.g., "what gifts for mom who has everything")
