## Playbook Overview

A full customer service operating manual for Family Gift Studio, covering pre-sale inquiries, order management, post-purchase support, and retention.

## Customer Profiles

Based on the 150 Holiday Gaming Customer Profiles research, FGS customers fall into key archetypes:

| Archetype | Motivation | Primary Concern |
|---|---|---|
| Last-Minute Giver | Convenience and speed | Delivery time guarantees |
| Sentimental Buyer | Emotional impact | Personalization quality |
| Budget-Conscious | Value and deals | Price transparency |
| Repeat Gifter | Loyalty and trust | Consistency |
| Gift Researcher | Confidence before buying | Reviews and proof |

## Response Templates

### Pre-Sale Inquiries
- Personalization options and limitations
- Production and delivery timelines
- Custom order requests
- Bulk/wholesale inquiries

### Order Management
- Order status and tracking updates
- Change requests within production window
- Rush order handling
- Address correction process

### Post-Purchase Support
- "Package damaged" resolution script
- "Not what I expected" empathy framework
- Return and exchange policy communication
- Reorder and loyalty follow-up

## Escalation Matrix

| Issue Type | Response Time | Resolution Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Damaged product | 2 hours | Customer Service Lead |
| Late delivery (holiday) | 1 hour | CS Lead + Operations |
| Personalization error | Same day | Production + CS |
| Refund request | 24 hours | CS Manager |
