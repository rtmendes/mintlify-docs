## Brand Identity

Family Gift Studio (FGS) is a personalized gifts and jewelry eCommerce brand built around emotional storytelling and meaningful gifting moments.

### Core Brand Values
- **Emotional Connection** — Every product tells a story
- **Personalization** — Unique, made-to-order pieces
- **Quality Craftsmanship** — Artisan-level attention to detail
- **Family First** — Celebrating relationships and milestones

### Brand Voice
- Warm, heartfelt, and authentic
- Conversational but elevated
- Empathetic and celebratory
- Never cold, clinical, or transactional

## Visual Identity

### Logo
The Love Knot M logo represents connection and continuity. See vectorization guidelines from the *Love Knot M Logo Design Vectorization* chat.

### Color Palette
| Color | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | Main CTAs, headers | High contrast, WCAG AA compliant |
| Secondary | Accents, highlights | Warm complementary tones |
| Neutral | Background, body text | Clean and readable |

<Note>Contrast visibility guidelines are documented in the *Contrast Visibility Fix* chat. Always verify AA compliance before publishing.</Note>

### Typography
- Headlines: Serif or elegant script for emotional warmth
- Body: Clean sans-serif for readability
- CTAs: Bold, action-oriented

### Photography Direction
Reference the *Diverse Lifestyle Hero Images* chat for photo style guidance:
- Authentic, diverse family representations
- Warm, natural lighting
- Real moments over staged poses
