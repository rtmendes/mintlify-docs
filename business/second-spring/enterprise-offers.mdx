## Enterprise Opportunity

The menopause wellness market represents an untapped enterprise opportunity. Organizations are facing increased awareness around menopause in the workplace (UK legislation, progressive HR policies in the US) with very few credentialed vendors to serve them.

**Market Reality:**
- 1 in 4 women considers leaving work due to menopause symptoms
- Fortune 500 companies lose ~$1.8B/year in lost productivity from menopause-related absenteeism
- Less than 5% of US corporations have a formal menopause workplace policy
