## Strategy Overview

A content-first approach to building Second Spring into the #1 authority brand for menopause wellness — combining educational content, community building, and content-to-commerce conversion funnels.

## The 7-Figure Content Framework

### Phase 1 — Authority Foundation (Months 1-3)
Build topical authority before monetizing:
- 30 cornerstone articles covering all major menopause symptoms
- 10 "Ultimate Guide" pillar pages (2,000+ words each)
- Expert interview series with OB-GYNs and integrative medicine practitioners
- Podcast launch: *Second Spring* (weekly, 30 min episodes)

### Phase 2 — Community & Email Growth (Months 4-6)
- Lead magnet: "The Menopause Relief Starter Kit" (PDF + email course)
- Email list target: 10,000 subscribers by month 6
- Private Facebook/community group: Second Spring Circle
- Weekly newsletter: *The Bloom* — research + practical tips

### Phase 3 — Content-to-Commerce (Months 7-12)
- Product-integrated content: "best supplements for X symptom"
- Case studies and transformation stories
- Affiliate partner content
- Revenue target: $1.7M ARR by month 12

## Editorial Calendar Structure

| Content Type | Frequency | Platform | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEO blog posts | 4/week | Website | Organic traffic |
| Email newsletter | Weekly | Email | Nurture + retention |
| Podcast episodes | Weekly | Spotify/Apple | Authority + reach |
| LinkedIn thought leadership | 5/week | LinkedIn | B2B/enterprise |
| Short-form video | 3/week | TikTok/Reels | Top-of-funnel |

## Content Pillars

1. **Symptoms & Solutions** — Science-backed content on menopause symptoms
2. **Lifestyle & Wellness** — Nutrition, sleep, exercise, stress management
3. **Mindset & Identity** — Second Spring as a positive life chapter
4. **Community Stories** — Real women, real transformations
5. **Expert Insights** — Interviews, research summaries, clinical perspectives

## Distribution Strategy
- Repurpose every long-form piece into: social clips, email section, podcast talking points
- Syndicate to Medium, LinkedIn Articles, and relevant health publications
- Build backlink partnerships through content swaps with complementary wellness brands
