## SEO Overview

Second Spring's SEO strategy targets the rapidly growing menopause wellness search market — high-intent, underserved, and dominated by generic health sites that lack brand depth.

## Keyword Strategy

### Tier 1 — Core Commercial Keywords
High-intent transactional queries directly tied to products and services:

| Keyword | Est. Monthly Volume | Difficulty | Intent |
|---|---|---|---|
| menopause supplements | High | Medium | Commercial |
| menopause weight loss | High | Medium | Informational → Commercial |
| brain fog menopause | Medium | Low | Informational |
| HRT alternatives natural | Medium | Low | Commercial |
| perimenopause symptoms | High | High | Informational |

### Tier 2 — Long-Tail & Symptom Keywords
Symptom-specific queries where Second Spring can build topical authority:
- "why do I feel foggy during menopause"
- "best supplements for menopause hot flashes"
- "perimenopause anxiety natural remedies"
- "menopause sleep problems solutions"

### Tier 3 — Authority/Credentialing Keywords
Queries that build brand trust and support enterprise positioning:
- "menopause specialist [city]"
- "certified menopause coach"
- "corporate menopause wellness program"

## Technical SEO Priorities
1. Core Web Vitals optimization (LCP < 2.5s)
2. Schema markup: MedicalCondition, FAQPage, Product
3. Mobile-first indexing compliance
4. Internal linking from symptom pages → product/service pages
5. E-E-A-T signals: author credentials, citations, medical review badges

## Link Building Strategy
- Guest posts on women's health publications
- Podcast appearances (menopause, women's wellness)
- Expert quotes for journalists (HARO/Qwoted)
- Partnerships with OB-GYN and integrative medicine practices
