## Campaign Overview

The Brain Fog Campaign is a LinkedIn-first content initiative addressing one of menopause's most professionally disruptive symptoms — cognitive fog. It targets professional women (35-55) who are high-performers experiencing unexplained cognitive decline.

**Campaign Scale:** 180 LinkedIn posts
**Duration:** 6 months (30 posts/month)
**Primary Platform:** LinkedIn
**Target Audience:** Professional women, HR leaders, C-suite executives

## Why Brain Fog for LinkedIn?

- Professional women are often ashamed of cognitive symptoms at work
- LinkedIn audiences are high-intent and receptive to wellness in professional contexts
- Brain fog as a topic bridges personal health → workplace productivity → enterprise wellness programs
- Creates a natural upsell path to Second Spring's enterprise corporate wellness offering
