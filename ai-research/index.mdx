# AI Research Lab

This is the unified research library combining outputs from **Genspark** (1,080 projects) and **Manus.ai** into a single, topic-organized knowledge base.

## Why one folder?

Both tools produce the same type of artifact: deep AI-generated research, plans, builds, and playbooks. Splitting them by source platform would scatter related ideas across separate silos. Here, **topic = folder** and **source platform = frontmatter tag**.

Every page in this section includes a `source` field in its frontmatter:

```yaml
---
source: genspark   # or: manus
---
```

Use this tag to filter by platform when you need to compare outputs or trace provenance.
